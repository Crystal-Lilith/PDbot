The Amazing Community

Programmer's Den is a discord server for people who are interested in programming! We accept people of all skill levels either expert or completely new at programming. This is a server with a community of programmers who helps each other out. We have a custom bot that allows you to create a category for your project with no permission limits within the category. You are able to add developers to it so that they may join your project. It would be your own private category that only people you allow can access! Feel free to socialize and meet new people who have the same interests. You might even meet new people to start awesome projects with!

Friendly Users!

Our server is full of friendly programmers! If you ever need help with your project, you can ask us for help! We have auto-assigning roles so that you can select the languages of your interest. However, if you are already experienced, you can also help other people who are wanting to learn and strive! We have dedicated channels for each programming language so that you can go more into detail about the language. You might even find new friends with common interests!

Active Staff!

We always have staff that is active and ready to help! They are very understanding don't be afraid to ask them questions or ask for help. We also accept suggestions from everyone so that we can improve! If you would like to be a part of our staff team, that is possible too!
